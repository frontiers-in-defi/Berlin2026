# Refund Policy

This policy applies to registrations for the **Frontiers in DeFi Conference 2026**.

## Registration Fees

Conference registration fees grant access to the conference event and related activities as specified during ticket purchase.

## Refunds

Participants may request a refund under the following conditions:

- Full refunds are available up to **14 days before the conference**.
- After this deadline, refunds may not be guaranteed due to event planning and fixed costs.

Refund requests must be submitted via email to:

conference@htw-berlin.de

## How Refunds Are Processed

Approved refunds will be processed through the original payment method via our payment provider **Stripe**.

Depending on the payment provider and bank, refunds may take **5–10 business days** to appear in your account.

## Ticket Transfers

If you cannot attend the conference, you may request a **ticket transfer to another participant**. Please contact us before the event.

## Event Cancellation

If the conference is cancelled by the organizers, all participants will receive a **full refund of the registration fee**.

## Force Majeure

The organizers are not responsible for delays or cancellations caused by circumstances beyond their control, including but not limited to:

- natural disasters
- government restrictions
- public health emergencies
- transportation disruptions

## Contact

For any refund-related questions, please contact:

conference@htw-berlin.de
