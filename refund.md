    <h1>Refund Policy</h1>

    <p>
      This policy applies to registrations for the <strong>Frontiers in DeFi Conference 2026</strong>.
    </p>

    <h2>Registration Fees</h2>
    <p>
      Conference registration fees grant access to the conference event and related activities as specified during ticket purchase.
    </p>

    <h2>Refunds</h2>
    <p>Participants may request a refund under the following conditions:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Full refunds are available up to <strong>14 days before the conference</strong>.</li>
      <li>After this deadline, refunds may not be guaranteed due to event planning and fixed costs.</li>
    </ul>

    <p>
      Refund requests must be submitted via email to:
      <a href="mailto:conference@htw-berlin.de">conference@htw-berlin.de</a>
    </p>

    <h2>How Refunds Are Processed</h2>
    <p>
      Approved refunds will be processed through the original payment method via our payment provider <strong>Stripe</strong>.
      Depending on the payment provider and bank, refunds may take <strong>5–10 business days</strong> to appear in your account.
    </p>

    <h2>Ticket Transfers</h2>
    <p>
      If you cannot attend the conference, you may request a <strong>ticket transfer to another participant</strong>.
      Please contact us before the event.
    </p>

    <h2>Event Cancellation</h2>
    <p>
      If the conference is cancelled by the organizers, all participants will receive a <strong>full refund of the registration fee</strong>.
    </p>

    <h2>Force Majeure</h2>
    <p>
      The organizers are not responsible for delays or cancellations caused by circumstances beyond their control, including but not limited to:
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li>natural disasters</li>
      <li>government restrictions</li>
      <li>public health emergencies</li>
      <li>transportation disruptions</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Contact</h2>
    <p>
      For any refund-related questions, please contact:
      <a href="mailto:conference@htw-berlin.de">conference@htw-berlin.de</a>
    </p>
  </div>
