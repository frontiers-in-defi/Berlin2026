# Privacy Policy

This website is operated for the **Frontiers in DeFi Conference 2026** hosted at  
Hochschule für Technik und Wirtschaft Berlin (HTW Berlin).

Protecting your personal data is very important to us. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how it is used in accordance with the **EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**.

## Responsible Entity

Frontiers in DeFi Conference 2026  
Hochschule für Technik und Wirtschaft Berlin  
Treskowallee 8  
10318 Berlin  
Germany  

Contact:  
conference@htw-berlin.de

## Data We Collect

When you register for the conference, we may collect the following information:

- Name
- Email address
- Affiliation / institution
- Payment information
- Ticket type and registration details

This data is required to manage conference registrations and communication.

## Registration and Ticketing

Conference registrations are handled through the ticketing platform **Pretix**.

Pretix processes registration data on our behalf in order to:

- manage ticket purchases
- issue confirmations
- generate invoices
- manage attendee lists

For more information, please refer to Pretix's privacy policy.

## Payment Processing

Payments are processed through **Stripe**.

Stripe may process payment data such as:

- payment method information
- billing address
- transaction details

We do not store full payment card details ourselves.

More information:  
https://stripe.com/privacy

## Use of Data

Personal data is used exclusively for:

- conference registration
- communication with participants
- issuing invoices and confirmations
- organizing the conference event

We do **not sell or share personal data with third parties** except where necessary to provide the services described above.

## Data Retention

Personal data will only be stored for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes listed above or as required by law.

Financial records may be retained longer to comply with accounting and tax regulations.

## Your Rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

- access your stored data
- request correction of inaccurate data
- request deletion of your data
- restrict processing
- withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise these rights, please contact us at:

conference@htw-berlin.de

## External Links

This website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those websites.

## Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if required by law or changes to the conference organization.
