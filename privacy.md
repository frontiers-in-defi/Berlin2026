    <h2>Data We Collect</h2>
    <p>When you register for the conference, we may collect the following information:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Name</li>
      <li>Email address</li>
      <li>Affiliation / institution</li>
      <li>Payment information</li>
      <li>Ticket type and registration details</li>
    </ul>

    <p>
      This data is required to manage conference registrations and communication.
    </p>

    <h2>Registration and Ticketing</h2>
    <p>
      Conference registrations are handled through the ticketing platform <strong>Pretix</strong>.
    </p>
    <p>Pretix processes registration data on our behalf in order to:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>manage ticket purchases</li>
      <li>issue confirmations</li>
      <li>generate invoices</li>
      <li>manage attendee lists</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Payment Processing</h2>
    <p>
      Payments are processed through <strong>Stripe</strong>.
    </p>
    <p>
      Stripe may process payment data such as payment method information, billing address, and transaction details.
      We do not store full payment card details ourselves.
    </p>

    <h2>Use of Data</h2>
    <p>Personal data is used exclusively for:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>conference registration</li>
      <li>communication with participants</li>
      <li>issuing invoices and confirmations</li>
      <li>organizing the conference event</li>
    </ul>

    <p>
      We do not sell or share personal data with third parties except where necessary to provide the services described above.
    </p>

    <h2>Data Retention</h2>
    <p>
      Personal data will only be stored for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes listed above or as required by law.
    </p>
    <p>
      Financial records may be retained longer to comply with accounting and tax regulations.
    </p>

    <p>Under the GDPR you have the right to:</p>
    <ul>

      <li>request correction of inaccurate data</li>
      <li>request deletion of your data</li>
      <li>restrict processing</li>
      <li>withdraw consent where applicable</li>
